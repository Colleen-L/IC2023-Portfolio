Math

My interest in math most likely stems from my mom. Because my mom was too old to attend high school when she immigrated to the United States, she went to school for her GED when I was about 8 years old. During that time, my mom would also teach me a little bit of what she was learning when we were at the restuarant. She taught me the times table, gave me challenge questions, and taught me a snippets of algebra. I learned to really enjoy the challenge and the concept of trying to solve problems.
